Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 31, 2026

Gemini Graph (Flowchart) Split-Viewer is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data and why we request specific permissions.

1. Data Collection and Privacy

We take a zero-data collection approach:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any other personal identifiers.
  • No Tracking: We do not track your browsing history, clicks, or any usage patterns.
  • Local Processing: All diagram rendering happens locally within your browser. Your Gemini chat content and diagrams are never sent to our servers or any third-party services.

2. Permissions Explained

Our extension requests the following permissions strictly for functional purposes:

  • Scripting: To inject the rendering engine and UI into the Gemini interface.
  • Tabs: To activate the extension instantly on open Gemini pages without requiring a refresh.
  • Storage: To remember your preferred UI settings (e.g., Side-by-Side vs Vertical mode).
  • Host Permissions (*://gemini.google.com/*): To restrict our extension's access solely to the Google Gemini domain.

3. Third-Party Services

The extension uses the open-source Mermaid.js library for rendering. This library runs entirely locally within the extension's environment.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
